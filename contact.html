Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Are any coins available for purchase? ▾
No. This is a personal collection maintained purely for educational and archival purposes. No coins are offered for sale through this website.
Can you help identify a coin I have found? ▾
We are happy to help where possible. Please send a clear photograph of both sides of the coin along with its approximate diameter and weight using the contact form above. We cannot guarantee identification of every coin but will do our best.
May I use your photographs for research or educational purposes? ▾
All photographs on this site are © The Indian Numismatic. Non-commercial educational use with attribution is generally permitted — please contact us first to discuss your needs.
How are the coins graded? ▾
Coins are graded using the standard Sheldon/PCGS scale adapted for world coins: Poor, Fair, About Good, Good, Very Good, Fine, Very Fine, Extremely Fine, About Uncirculated, and Uncirculated. All grades are the collector's own assessment unless otherwise noted.
Can I contribute information or corrections? ▾
Absolutely — and we welcome it. If you spot an error in attribution, dating, or description, or have additional historical context to share, please write to us. Corrections and contributions from fellow researchers are acknowledged on the relevant entry.
